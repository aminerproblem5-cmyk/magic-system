The Four Core Elements

Every Mage signs into the registry to harness one of the natural planetary flows. Each element channels differently through a user's Magic Power (MP):

🔥 Pyromancy (Plasma Flow)

Destructive, high-energy output. Pyromancers often post volatile MP fluctuations on the leaderboard, trading stability for raw speed.

⚡ Chronomancy (Time Distortion)

The art of slowing down intervals. High-ranking Chronomancers are rumor-bound to freeze their trial parameters to secure consistent rolls.

🌌 Void Attunement

Manipulating dark matter. Void users focus on gravity wells, allowing them to anchor their scores so their hierarchy standing rarely drops.

🌿 Aether Healing

Channelling planetary rejuvenation. This magic allows council members to grant licenses and stabilize corrupt profile matrices.
