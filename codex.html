The Five Great Mancies

A Structured Codex of Core, Sub, and Micro-Sub Elements

The Law of the Focus Slate

Magic cannot be conjured from nothing. To manifest the energies of the Great Mancies, a caster must possess a physically prepared conduit known as a Focus Slate. Without the stone and its inscription, the magic remains locked.

Dimensions & Material

Every Slate must be meticulously cut from natural, magically conductive rock. The standard legal dimension for a slate is exactly 2.5 inches by 3.5 inches (6.3 cm by 8.8 cm) with a thickness of roughly a quarter-inch, allowing it to easily fit in the palm or a caster's pouch.

The Sacred Inscription

To pull from a specific branch of magic, the slate must be deeply carved with the exact, mathematically correct symbol representing that sub or micro-element. An error as small as a hairline fracture in the carving will cause the spell to misfire or fail entirely.

1. Pyromancy

Thermomancy (Heat Control)

  • Combustion: Forcing objects or air pockets to instantly hit ignition point.
  • Cryo-Extraction: Stealing heat rapidly from an object to drop it to absolute zero.
  • Plasma-weaving: Superheating gas until it turns into a glowing blade or orb of pure energy.

Electromancy (Lightning)

  • Arc-Striking: Summoning and redirecting raw bolts of lightning from atmospheric friction.
  • Galvanism: Manipulating the internal electrical signals within a target's nervous system.
  • Magnetism: Utilizing electrical currents to generate fields that pull or repel metallic structures.

Radiamancy (Light & Radiation)

  • Solar Flares: Creating blinding bursts of light capable of searing skin and burning vision.
  • Hard-Light: Solidifying light waves into temporary, transparent shields or platforms.
  • Gamma Decay: Projecting invisible waves of radioactive heat that slowly dissolve material structures.

2. Aeromancy

Baromancy (Pressure)

  • Vacuum Folds: Ripping air away from an area to choke out flames or suffocate a foe.
  • Shockwave: Compressing air heavily and releasing it in a massive kinetic explosion.
  • Buoyancy: Decreasing air resistance around oneself to achieve effortless flight or high speed.

Phonomancy (Sound)

  • Acoustic Resonance: Projecting targeted frequencies that shatter glass, stone, and bone.
  • Dead Zone: Vibrating air molecules to cancel out sound entirely for absolute stealth.
  • Echolocation: Bouncing undetectable sound waves through an environment to map it completely.

Atmospherics (Weather Chaos)

  • Cyclonogenics: Spinning local currents to build localized tornadoes and gale-force wind walls.
  • Mist-Weaving: Condensing moisture in the air into thick, blinding cloaks of fog.
  • Tempest Call: Forcing low and high pressures to collide, instantly turning clear skies into storms.

3. Necromancy

Osteomancy (Bone & Structure)

  • Marrow Shards: Extracting and hardening skeletal structures into razor-sharp projectiles.
  • Calcification: Over-growing or solidifying bones to act as external armor plating.
  • Skeletal Animation: Forcing dead skeletal structures to obey commands through raw kinetic magic.

Spiritism (Soul Bending)

  • Wraith Summoning: Ripping violent, lingering echoes of the deceased back into the material realm.
  • Astral Tethering: Forcibly parting a living soul from its body for short durations.
  • Possession: Shoving a stray spirit into an object or living thrall to take control.

Miasmamancy (Decay & Plagues)

  • Putrefaction: Accelerating the natural decomposition process of organic matters instantly.
  • Virulence: Sprouting toxic spores and airborne contagions that drain physical stamina.
  • Parasitic Siphon: Leeching vital life-force from a dying target to repair the caster's injuries.

4. Geomancy

Terramancy (Stone & Earth)

  • Seismic Shifting: Generating powerful vibrations through the ground to create tremors.
  • Psammomancy: Reducing solid rock into shifting swarms of sand to choke or blind targets.
  • Litho-shaping: Pulling pillars, walls, or spikes directly out of any stone terrain.

Ferromancy (Metal)

  • Metallurgy: Liquefying, reshaping, and sharpening raw or refined metals at will.
  • Mercury Fluidity: Controlling liquid metal alloys for versatile, shape-shifting combat gear.
  • Magnetoreception: Sensing buried veins of metal deep underground like a radar.

Crystallomancy (Gems)

  • Prism Spikes: Growing dense, diamond-hard crystal spears rapidly out of any surface.
  • Refraction Shell: Generating a crystal barrier that reflects magical beams back at attackers.
  • Resonance Storage: Charging crystals with elemental magic to act as explosive or glowing traps.

5. Hydromancy

Aquamancy (Pure Liquid)

  • Tidal Kinetics: Controlling massive volumes of water to create crashing waves and whirlpools.
  • Pressure Jet: Condensing a stream of water so fine and pressurized it cuts through solid steel.
  • Aquatic Shaping: Creating physical tools, bubbles, or ropes out of surface water.

Hemomancy (Blood)

  • Puppetry: Taking control of the blood currents within a living body to force physical movement.
  • Coagulation: Drawing blood out of wounds and instant-hardening it into razor-sharp blades.
  • Anemia: Drawing blood away from a target's brain to cause instant blackout or disorientation.

Acidmancy (Toxins & Corrosion)

  • Caustic Spray: Exuding highly acidic, fizzing liquids that melt through shields and armor.
  • Neurotoxin: Transmuting clean liquid into precise poisons that target the nervous system.
  • Alchemical Solvents: Creating liquids designed to safely dissolve specific magical enchantments.
